PASHU PEHCHAN

AI CATTLE BREED IDENTIFICATION

Pashu Pehchan was designed to digitize and automate cattle breed identification for agricultural use. The platform required a seamless operational bridge between a consumer-facing mobile web interface and a heavy backend machine learning inference service.

The architecture securely streams heavy media payloads directly to a FastAPI microservice for classification. Once the ML engine returns its confidence scores, a fully formatted diagnostic PDF report is dynamically constructed on-the-fly. The system features an aggregation-driven dashboard to visualize historical scan data and demographic trends.

MERN YOLOv8 EfficientNet PDF Generation

EYE ONSITE

SMART CONSTRUCTION SAFETY SYSTEM

EyeOnSite tackles critical safety blindspots on active construction sites by replacing manual monitoring with intelligent, real-time computer vision.

The platform utilizes a YOLO/ByteTrack pipeline that persistently analyzes live camera feeds to detect PPE violations, hazardous machinery proximity, and precise zone intrusions while actively filtering out false alerts. To empower site managers, the system provides an interactive hazard zone editor, predictive collision warnings, and a centralized live dashboard, dramatically reducing response times to potential workplace accidents.

CLINIX

CONFLICT-FREE CLINIC APPOINTMENT SYSTEM

Clinix bridges the gap between patient accessibility and clinical management by eliminating scheduling conflicts and disjointed healthcare records through a unified, role-based architecture.

Emphasizing inclusive and intelligent care, the system features map-integrated specialty doctor discovery and multilingual symptom structuring to break language barriers. Additionally, an AI-powered medical report summarization tool distills complex histories, enabling rapid physician review and drastically reducing administrative overhead.

MERN AI Role-Based Auth Maps API

HELIX

GENE-GUIDED PRESCRIBING PLATFORM

HELIX was engineered to solve a critical bottleneck in precision medicine: the automated interpretation of raw genomic data against clinical protocols. The core challenge was designing a deterministic pipeline that could ingest massive VCF files, extract specific pharmacogenomic variants, and reconstruct patient diplotypes with 100% accuracy.

The system strictly validates variant data against JSON schemas before piping it through a CPIC rule engine to calculate medication risk profiles. To bridge the gap between genomic outputs and clinician readability, a fine-tuned LLM acts as a translator, generating actionable prescribing adjustments while maintaining strict clinical reliability.

MERN Genomics CPIC AI

SEVAMITRA

CROWDSOURCED CIVIC ISSUE REPORTING

SevaMitra transforms disjointed urban complaint systems into a streamlined, crowdsourced civic resolution pipeline, bridging the communication gap between citizens and municipal authorities.

It empowers users to instantly report local infrastructure and utility issues natively from the street via high-precision GPS tagging and structured image uploads. On the operational side, the platform provisions a comprehensive tracking dashboard designed specifically for NGOs and officials to actively manage, triage, and transparently resolve civic infrastructure problems.

MERN GPS/Maps Image Upload Dashboard

LINKRYTHM

DYNAMIC CREATOR PROFILE PLATFORM

LinkRythm redefines digital identity management by providing a highly dynamic, context-aware creator profile platform, moving beyond static link lists to optimize audience conversion rates.

The backend-driven infrastructure executes intelligent, device- and location-based link prioritization. Supported by a highly scalable REST architecture, the platform automatically determines smart link ordering and generates unique, trackable QR codes, equipping creators with automated control over traffic redirection and their interactive digital presence.
